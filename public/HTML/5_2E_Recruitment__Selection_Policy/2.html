
Employee Referrals

A
permanent
employee
may
refer
individuals
who
fit
the
specifications given in the job description for vacant positions which
Human Resources team may post from time to time. In case the
resume is already available in the data bank, through another source,
the referral process will be terminated at this stage.

If the referred candidate is hired, the referrer is eligible to claim a
referral fee as per the parameters defined below.
Referral reward:
Experience/ Position Range
Referral Amount
Team Leader/ Manager
Rs. 10,000/-
Executive
Rs. 4,000/-
Any exceptions can be discussed on a case to case basis.
TERMS & CONDITIONS

The Referral fee will be paid in two parts along with the salary.

After the successful completion of probation period of the referred
candidate 50% referral fee shall be paid and the remaining 50% shall
be disbursed upon completion of one year of service.

The referral fee shall be given only if the new recruit successfully
completes the above mentioned time period with the organization.

The referrer must be employed with SIPI for this payment to be
made. The payment will become due to the referrer on the next
regular payroll date.

The referral amount being a taxable component will be paid after
deducting TDS, as applicable.
Note :
•
Fee amounts and eligibility factors are subject to change at any time
based on the business and economic needs of SIPI.

An Ex-Employee of SIPI or any of the Group Companies will not be
considered as a referral under this policy
