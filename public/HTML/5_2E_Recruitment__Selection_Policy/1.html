Objective
To resource the right talent through Internal and External sources to
achieve the Business Objectives and Goals of the Company.
Applicability
It is applicable for hiring of employees
Policy Content
GUIDELINES

Vacant position will be filled by the company through External or
Internal sources of recruitment. In case of external sources, company
may use any of the following sources: Job portals, employment
agencies,
direct
application
by
candidates
(including
Office
Reception), advertisement, Networking sites (LinkedIn), campus
placements, employee referrals, etc.

In addition to the external sources, company may also consider
internal sourcing.

Candidates who have been interviewed by DIPI will not be eligible to
apply within 6 months from his/ her last interview.

Recruitment would be on the basis of Manpower requirement
identified by the HOD who then fills the Manpower Requisition form
duly approved by concerned persons as mentioned in the form.
1.1 Recruitment Process:
Recruitment Process includes 2 steps:
a)
Sourcing (External and Internal), and
b)
Shortlisting/ Selecting
I)
External Sourcing

All vacancies in the company which are to be filled externally are
normally sourced through predetermined recruitment consultants,
Job Portals, Direct Application, campus recruitments, advertisement,
employee referrals, etc.
