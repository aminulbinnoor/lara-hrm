Objective

To ensure proper assessment of
an employee’s fitment for a given role in
the company;

To ensure a defined probation period for employees of each Grade;

To ensure defined guidelines to be fo
llowed for employee’s confirmation
or probation extension.
Applicability
This policy is applicable to all the employees of the Company.
This policy will not apply to contractual personnel, consultants and personnel
who are not on the rolls of the Company
.
Policy Content
Probation means an initial review period during which an employee is
evaluated on a range of parameters with the objective of assessing his/ her
suitability for a given role.
Probation Duration:
6 months
Probation Extension:
1-3 Months
Probation Review Process
a)
HR Department shall initiate the process of probation completion 15 days
before the date of completion and intimate the
employee’s
Supervisor
and respective HOD for filling the Probation Review Form.
b)
Supervisor and HOD reviews the performance of the employee and
accordingly fills the Probation Review Form at least 10 days before the
completion of the period of probation.
c)
Supervisor and HOD, will decide either of the followings:

To confirm the employee, if the performance is satisfactory

To allow one time extension of 1-3 months, if the performance is
not adequate

If the performance is not adequate even post extension, the
employee will be terminated
