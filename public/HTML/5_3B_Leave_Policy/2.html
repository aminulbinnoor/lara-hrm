Human Resources Policies
Sick Leave

An employee shall be entitled to Sick Leave of 6 days in a year which
will be credited at the beginning of the year and will be calculated on
a pro-rata basis for the new employees.

Unable to attend duties on the ground of sickness, employee can
avail the sick leave with proper intimation to the reporting officer.

For Sick Leave of 2 or more consecutive days, the leave application
must be supported by a medical certificate indicating period of
sickness and nature of illness. On joining back, fitness certificate is to
be submitted to the HR.

Sick leave cannot be encashed.

Any sick leave availed in excess of the entitlement will be adjusted
from the CL. In case sufficient CL balance is unavailable, sick leaves
would be treated as leave without pay.
Special Leave
–
Marriage/Bereavement leave
All employees are eligible to claim special leave:
I.
for a period not exceeding 10 working days to attend unfortunate
event- death of a family member*, or
II.
for a period not exceeding 15 working days for self-marriage (only
first time marriage)
approved by the management.
Note:
(i)
All special leaves shall be subject to the approval of HR head along
with the respective HOD.
(ii)
A gift voucher of INR 5000 in case of marriage to employees.
(iii)
Employees who have been with the company for a period of at least
90 days in the 12 months preceding the wedding are entitled for the
marriage leave.
* Family member shall imply spouse, children, parents and unmarried
siblings only.
Maternity Leave/ Paternity Leave

No female employee shall be entitled to maternity benefit unless she
has worked in the company for a period of not less than 80 days in
the 12 months immediately preceding the date of her expected
delivery.

Employees are required to indicate to HR in writing, the date on
which they wish their maternity leave to begin. A medical certificate
giving the probable date of delivery should be attached with the
leave application.

After resuming work a medical certificate giving the actual date of
delivery is to be submitted.

In case of any deviation from the above policy, written approval has
to be obtained from the HOD and Admin Head.
