Objective
1. To ensure that the employees are able to attend to their personal
contingencies and to promote work life balance.
2. To have better leave management system in the company.
3. To define the type/ nature of leaves to be taken by the employees;
4. To define the eligibility criteria for all employees.
Applicability
This policy shall apply to all on-roll employees of DIPI
Specific Rules
1.
Leave types
The various types of leaves are:

Casual Leave (CL)

Sick Leave (SL)

Special leave

Maternity/ Paternity Leave
Note: An employee under probation period can avail all the leaves
entitled to him.
2.
Employee entitlement for Leaves
Employees are entitled for the following leave with pay during a
calendar year:-

Casual Leave
:
24 working Days

Sick Leave
:
06 working Days

Marriage Leave
:
15 working Days

Bereavement leave
:
10 working Days

Maternity Leave
:
3 months

Paternity Leave
:
15 working days
Casual Leave (CL)

All employees are entitled to 24 days of casual leave per year.

CL will be calculated on a pro-rata basis for a new employee.

CL cannot be availed in fractions, i.e. less than a day. Thus, the
minimum CL that can be taken by an employee is 1 day.

Un-availed Casual Leave at the end of year will carried forward to the
next year and upto 8 days leave will be carried forward to the next
year.
