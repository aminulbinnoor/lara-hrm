Objective
To ensure that the policy sets out to identify how the company executes its
duty to keep the company’s information secure and confidential. All staff
employed by the company have a duty to:

Keep such information strictly confidential and to use it only for the
proper purposes in accordance with the terms of engagement;

Ensure that confidential information is accessed only on a strict need-to-
know basis;

Ensure that everyone with access to confidential information is aware
about their responsibilities
This policy also guides you on the measures to be taken while handling
confidential papers or information.
Applicability
All employees of the company
Policy content
Confidential
–
For the purpose of this policy, confidential shall include all such
information which is not publicly available to the recipient of such information.
Confidential information shall comprise of the following:
1.
Personnel Specific
: This comprises of all information about the employees
which is personal in nature
–
Family background, Education Qualification,
Past Health Records, Appraisal related information etc.
2.
Company Specific
: This comprises of all such information which is
pertaining to the Company only
–
trade secrets, business decisions, internal
news and policies, any other information not meant for public in general.
Utmost care must be taken to ensure that none of the confidential information
of the company (e.g. Policies, compensation etc.) is revealed or shared with
people, whether within the company or outside, who are not authorized by the
company to be aware of the confidential information.
You are reminded of certain sensible measures which you should take to be
sure that we never breach this confidence:

Never carry papers or files outside the office without first placing them in
a brief case or a secure cover;

If any papers are to be delivered to a client/ vendor, they should be
placed in an envelope with a transmittal letter. The envelope should be
sealed;

