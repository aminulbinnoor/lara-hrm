Objective
1.To define a dress code for employees to build the image of the
Company;
2.The dress code policy developed to maintain and promote high
standards of personal appearance, hygiene and professionalism at the
work place
Applicability
This policy shall apply to all on-roll employees of DIPI
Specific Rules
BUSINESS ATTIRE
MONDAY TO THRUSDAY
1.
Do’s for Men
: Business Suits (optional), Formal Trousers, Blazers
(optional), collared shirts, Ties (optional), closed formal shoes.
2.
Do’s for Women
: Sarees, Salwar, Kameez, Business Suits,
Collared shirts, shoes and sandals.
FRIDAY TO SATURDAY
1.
DOS for Men & Women: Business Casuals, collared T-shirts,
Khakis, corduroy etc
2.
Don't for Men & Women: Torn Jeans, slippers, shorts, round
collar t-shirt and tracksuits, Kurta & Pajama (Kurta& Pajama only
on traditional
day’s
celebration).
*ALL
EMPLOYEES
DEALING
FACE
TO
FACE
WITH
EXTERNAL
CUSTOMERS MUST WEAR NORMAL BUSINESS ATTIRE (SAME AS
MONDAY TO THRUSDAY)
General
Guidelines
1.
We trust all employees will exercise good judgment in their dress
sense to upkeep our corporate image.
2.
Wherever the activities / role of the employee stipulate them to
wear a specific set of cloths / uniform the same needs to be
followed.
3.
The employee will dress in business casuals as specified above on
workings Saturday. However, this rule would not apply if there is
an external business meeting schedule on workings Saturdays.
