Human Resources Policies
It is the Company's policy to comply fully with all applicable laws and regulations

X. Workplace Dignity and Respect
The DIPI's diverse workforce is one of our greatest strengths. Bringing together and
supporting employees from different backgrounds, perspectives and cultures helps
create a dynamic business that reflects the range of customers we serve.
Therefore the DIPI is committed to providing equal opportunities for all its
employees and job applicants who will receive fair treatment regardless of age,
race, religion or belief, colour, nationality, ethnic or national origin, sex, sexual
orientation, gender reassignment, marital status or disability. It is our aim that all
relationships in the workplace are free from discrimination, bias and harassment.
You must ensure that you do not, by your own actions, behaviour or attitude,
directly or indirectly discriminate against job applicants or fellow employees. In
some countries employees can be held personally liable for acts of discrimination
they commit.
Workplace dignity and respect: It’s Everyone’s responsibility
DIPI is committed to providing a fair working environment where all employees are
treated with dignity and respect. Company will not tolerate any form of bullying
and harassment and it will take all practicable steps to avoid and eliminate this. Any
form of bullying and/or harassment will be regarded as gross misconduct and may
result in disciplinary action including dismissal.
The policy is based on the following key principles:

The responsibility
to ensure that all employees are treated equally and
fairly in the workplace

The right of employees to be treated with dignity and respect and to work
without fear of harassment on any grounds.

The responsibility of all employees to prevent any acts that may constitute
harassment from being undertaken either by themselves or by others.
We aim to encourage our employees to build cultures in which respect for
individuals is regarded as an essential part of the conduct of all those who work in
our organization and employees are valued and
where there is no unethical
