D.
Payment of bill
•
The payment of an internet data card bill shall be made by the company
provided the monthly usage by an employee is within the limits defined
under monthly usage plan. In case, the monthly bill exceeds the limits
defined under monthly usage plan while misusing an internet data card for
personal use, then an amount over and above the monthly bill shall be paid
by an employee.
E.
On Separation/ Theft/ Loss/ Damage

In case of separation, the employee will be required to refund the
reimbursed amount of the Laptop and/or internet data card. For laptop, the
deduction will be on pro-rata basis (100% at any time during the first and
50% at any subsequent year). And for an internet data card, deduction will
be 100% at any first year & 50% at any time during the second year.

If a Company-owned laptop and/or internet data card is stolen, employee is
expected to immediately file an FIR with the local police and file a theft
report with all details as to the time, date, location and any other details
with HR and IT Department.
F.
Laptop Battery and AC Adapter Replacement

The Company may as required replace a battery that is out of warranty.

Employees are responsible for replacing lost, stolen, or damaged batteries,
and AC adapters.
G.
Declaration
Each employee shall sign a standard declaration, in the format as per Annexure A
below, to the above policy as to the acceptance of the policy.
H.
Employee’s departure from the
Company

Employee must return the laptop & internet data card to the Company on
or before their ending date of employment.

Failure to return the laptop may result in withholding payments such as Full
&Final settlement until the laptop is returned.
