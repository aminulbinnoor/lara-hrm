Objective

To ensure that the Company provides an appropriate IT hardware for all the
eligible Employees.
Applicability

As per Eligibility mentioned in the policy
Policy
Content
A.
Eligibility & Limit

Assistant Manager & above will be eligible for laptop and internet data
card/dongle. Laptops are authorized based upon job responsibilities,
demonstrated need, and departmental approvals.

Laptops & Internet data cards can also be issued to employees (below
Assistant Manager) on need basis post approval of HOD and Manager
Admin.
B.
Laptop usage

The laptops shall be used as per the usage Policy formulated by the IT
Department.
C.
Responsibility

It is the responsibility of employees to take appropriate precautions to
prevent damage to or loss/theft of laptop & internet data card in their care.

When an employee takes the laptop & internet data card out of his/her
office, he/she is expected to keep them in hand or sight, or in a secure and
locked location, at all times.

During the period, when the owner carries the Laptop & internet data card
with them the same should not be misused for the purpose of transferring
the data on to other storage devices. If any employee were found
malpractice, he/she would be liable for disciplinary action from the
organization.

No external devices to be used to copy data from the Laptop to transmit /
transfer to other agencies while on work at their site unless it is absolute
necessary Ex. use of DVDs, Pen Drives, and Network Drives at other location
and CDs.

Employees will be responsible for certain costs to repair or replace the
computer if the damage or loss is due to negligence or intentional
