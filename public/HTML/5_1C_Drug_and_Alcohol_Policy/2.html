B.
Firm’s right to conduct a drug test
The firm may ask an employee to submit to a drug and or/ alcohol test
at any time if it feels that the employee may be under the influence of
drugs or alcohol, including, but not limited to, the following
circumstances:

Evidence of consumption of drugs or alcohol;

Evidence of drugs or alcohol on or about the employee’s part that
suggests impairment or influence of drugs or alcohol;

Unusual conduct on the employee’s part that suggests impairment
or influence of drugs or alcohol;

Negative performance patterns or excessive and unexplained
absenteeism or tardiness;

Any employee involved in an on-the-job accident or injury under the
circumstances that suggest possible use or influence of drugs or
alcohol
