Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to ensure that the Company is able to:

Show its responsibility and commitment to ensure a safe and
healthy workplace for all employees

Outline its expectations and requirements for creating and
maintaining an alcohol and a drug free work environment, and for
dealing with substance abuse in the workplace.
Applicability
This policy applies to the employees of the firm at the workplace, at
client location and at the entire firm off sites/ events during and outside
of normal scheduled working hours.
Policy content
The Drug and Alcohol Policy shall be read along with the applicable
approved standing orders and all the prevailing laws as per the Indian
Statute.
A.
Prohibited acts
1.
The firm explicitly prohibits:

The use, possession, solicitation or sale of narcotics or other illegal
drugs, alcohol or medication without a prescription;

Being impaired under the influence of legal or illegal drugs or alcohol,
if such impairment or influence adversely affects the employee’s
work performance, the safety of the employee or of others or put
firm’s reputation
at risk;

Possession, use, solicitation or sale of legal or illegal drugs or alcohol,
if such activity or involvement adversely
affects the employee’s work
performance, the safety of the employee or of others, or puts the
firm’s reputation at risk

The presence of any detectable amount of prohibited substances in
employee’s system while at work, while on the premises of the firm,
client, any event/ off-
sites or while on firm’s business. “Prohibited
substances” include illegal drugs, alcohol, or prescription drugs not
taken in accordance with a prescription given to the employee.
2.
Involvement with alcohol or drugs during or outside the office hours
