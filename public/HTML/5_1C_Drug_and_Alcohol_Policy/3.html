Human Resources Policies
D.
Policy administration
Please find below some guidance on how to administer this policy. This
is just an indicative list of situations

If an employee, visitor or vendor arrives at the workplace and you
have reasonable cause to suspect that they are under the influence
of alcohol or drugs, the HOD/ HR Manager shall immediately remove
him/ her from the work environment

Unexpected circumstances can arise when an off-duty employee is
requested to work. It is the employee’s responsibility to refuse the
request and ask that the request be directed to another person if
the employee feels undue to the influence of alcohol or other drugs.

Employees who are prescribed medication are expected to ask their
doctor if the employee feels unfit due to the influence of alcohol or
other drugs.

Employees who are prescribed medication are expected to ask their
doctor if the medication will have any potential negative effect on
job performance. They are required to report to their supervisor if
there is any potential risk, limitation or restriction for whatever
reason that may require modification of duties or temporary
reassignment and provide appropriate medical verification on any
restrictions in performance of their duties.
If an employee believes that any senior is in violation of this policy, they
are encouraged to report the violation directly to HR Manager.
E.
Complaint redressal
On receiving a complaint, the following measure will be taken:

The respective HOD should be informed within 24 hrs, via written
communication.

HOD and HR Manager will convene and discuss within 24 hrs, way
forward for the specific case.

HOD and HR Manager to hold discussion with the complainant to
establish facts of the incidence and circulate the same to COO.

On establishing the occurrence of the incidence cited, the HOD and
HR Manager to speak with the offender to get his/ her clarification
on the allegation.

In case of tests to be administered, the same should be conducted
within 24 hours of establishing the occurrence of the incidence.

HOD and HR Manager to submit the findings along with the evidence
(if any), recommendation on way forward to COO within 3 days of
receiving the complaint.
