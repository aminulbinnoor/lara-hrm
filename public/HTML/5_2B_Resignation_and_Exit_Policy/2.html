2
Human Resource Policies
B.
Exit Interview
1.
Exit interview will be held with the exiting employee on or before his / her last
working day.
2.
HR Manager will interview the exiting employee. The interview should be
open-ended and be conducted in an informal atmosphere where the following
information is elicited:
-
About the new job
-
Reasons for leaving
-
Employee’s likes and dislikes regarding his
reporting manager, work and
the Company.
-
His views about the organizational culture & working atmosphere
-
Interaction with employees
-
Suggestions for improvement
Note: Please refer Annexure 1 for the Exit Interview Form

The interview may be conducted in a way where both the HR and the
employee feel comfortable and the maximum information can be elicited.

Data collected through exit interviews will be compiled by the HR Department.
A six monthly report shall be presented to the Management.
C.
Exit formalities

The HOD shall ensure that all the
Company’s
Data (viz.: Records, Documents,
Handover of an ongoing assignment etc.) allocated Assets have been duly
obtained

Clearance shall be obtained in
‘No Dues
Form
’
from concerned departments
and submitted to HR.

HR will then perform the relieving & clearance of the employee with Full &
Final settlement.
