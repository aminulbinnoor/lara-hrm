Objective

To ensure that proper feedback is obtained from the exiting employee
about the Company’s working and other things in order to
maintain smooth
operations.
Applicability

All onroll employees and workers of the Company
Policy content
A.
Notice period & relieving

When an employee resigns, the Supervisor/ HOD should attempt to
understand his reasons and identify means to retain the team member.

In case, there is no possibility of retaining the team member, the same shall be
intimated to the HR so as to commence the exit process.

The notice period to be served will be 2 months. Any shortfall shall be adjusted
in F&F settlement unless waived off by Director.

During the notice period it is essential that the necessary project related work/
information is transferred to another assigned team member.
B.
Clearance formalities

The exiting employee must comply with the clearance formalities as listed
in the Exit Clearance Form (refer Annexure 2)

The exiting employee shall be responsible for the completeness of the
clearance form.
C.
Full & final settlement

GA and HR Department will process F&F and forward it to accounts for
payment.

The same shall be completed within four weeks from last working day subject
to completion of No Dues form by the employee.
Procedure
A.
Acceptance of resignation letter
1.
Receipt & Acceptance of Letter of Resignation shall be done by the HOD.
2.
On acceptance of the resignation letter by the concerned person, the
exiting employee shall be intimated by a Letter of Acceptance issued by the
HR.
