Objective
To ensure that new employees are introduced to DIPI in a positive way in
line with good employment practice
Applicability
All employees on the roll of the company
General
guidelines
Induction is a systematic process, which enables new employees to become
familiar with their job, the Company, and the other people with whom they
will be working.
Procedure
1.
The induction process must be duly complied with. The HR Manager
shall be responsible for the complete compliance with the induction
process (refer in Annexure 1).
2.
The employee shall be required to fill-in a comprehensive checklist (refer
Annexure 2) within 45 days from the date of joining. The HR must
ensure that the same is duly filled.
3.
The Induction Program Checklists shall be maintained by the HR
Department.
4.
The HR Department shall ensure that the induction for all employees is
carried on for the requisite number of days as decided by the HOD and
the HR.

For any clarification regarding any aspect of the policy, kindly contact the
GA and HR Department.
